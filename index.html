Agricultural Visualisations

Agricultural Production Map

This map visualizes the total agricultural commodity production in Australia from 1912 to 2022. It provides a comprehensive overview of how production varies across different regions and commodities.

Commodity Production Radar Chart

The radar chart compares production levels of different commodities for a selected region and year. It highlights the variety of crops produced and their relative quantities.

Production and Hectares

The bubble chart illustrates the relationship between the production of various commodities and the hectares used for cultivation.

Agricultural Production Line Chart

This line chart shows agricultural production trends over the years for selected commodities.

Agricultural Production Scatter Plot

The scatter plot shows the correlation between production value and hectares for different commodities.
